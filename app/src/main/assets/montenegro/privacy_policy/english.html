Statement on Online Payment Security and Protection of Confidential Transaction Data

The confidentiality of user personal data is protected and secured during online payments. Monteput d.o.o. uses an advanced system for secure acceptance of payment cards over the Internet. All data is encrypted and transmitted from the web browser to the bank, and Monteput d.o.o. never comes into contact with the complete user payment card information.

The payment data entry form is secured with the highest reliability SSL transport encryption. All stored data is additionally protected by encryption using a cryptographic device. The Payment Gateway bank meets all online payment security requirements prescribed by the leading card brands, operating in compliance with the PCI DSS standard - the highest security standard of the payment card industry. The bank considers all collected information confidential and treats it accordingly. The information is used exclusively for the purposes for which it is intended. All user data is considered sensitive and is fully secure, with their privacy protected by the most modern protection mechanisms. Only the data necessary for conducting business in accordance with the prescribed demanding procedures for online payment are collected. Security controls and operational procedures applied to our infrastructure ensure the system's immediate reliability. Additionally, by maintaining strict access control, regularly monitoring security, and conducting in-depth checks to prevent network vulnerabilities, as well as systematically enforcing information security provisions, we continually maintain and improve the security level of the system by protecting card data.

The security of payment data is guaranteed by the payment card processor, NLB Bank, so the entire payment process takes place on the bank's pages, and the payment card data is not available to the Monteput d.o.o. system.
