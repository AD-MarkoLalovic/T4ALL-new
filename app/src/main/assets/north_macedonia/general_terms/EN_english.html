General Terms and Conditions

The use of the TOLL FOR ALL application through the web portal https://toll4all.com. is subject to the general terms and conditions of online application usage (hereinafter: General Terms and Conditions).

Users of the TOLL FOR ALL application are individuals and legal entities who meet the criteria prescribed by the General Terms and Conditions, are familiar with and fully accept the General Terms and Conditions, and have given consent to the processing of personal data after registration and providing the necessary personal data (user's name and surname, user's address, email address, phone number).

Description of Service and Products

Through the website https://toll4all.com., the Public Enterprise State Roads of North Macedonia will provide electronic toll collection services, including toll payment services incurred within the territory of the Republic of North Macedonia, within the Toll For All project.

Registered users of the TOLL FOR ALL application will have access to:
- Payment of tolls through electronic toll collection,
- Creating a personal user account and viewing all transactions with usage data of the TOLL FOR ALL device,
- Control of toll service expenses by monitoring transactions and current consumption,
- Archive of all previously issued invoices, displaying individual pass specifications on the registered TOLL FOR ALL device.

Payment Methods

Payment for services through the TOLL FOR ALL application can be made using payment cards - VISA, MasterCard, or other cards that support online payment. Card payment is carried out in cooperation with Halk Bank and is done in a secure and certified manner through the IPG (Internet Payment Gateway) by entering the data from the payment card.

For toll payment service, all registered users of the TOLL FOR ALL application will be automatically debited daily from the account of the registered payment card for the amount of the incurred debt.

The user is responsible for verifying with the card issuing bank whether the card they possess supports online payments.

Conversion Statement

All payments through the TOLL FOR ALL application will be made in denars, while for informational display of prices in other currencies, the average selling rate of the National Bank of North Macedonia is used. Your payment card will be charged for the amount obtained by converting your local currency according to the middle exchange rate of the National Bank of North Macedonia. As a result of this conversion, there may be a slight difference in the amount charged to you compared to the price shown on our website.

Appeals Procedure

The complaint procedure for an online transaction related to the use of the electronic toll collection service starts with contacting the Customer Support Service of Public Enterprise State Roads at the phone number +389-0800-800-88 or by submitting a request on the portal https://etc.roads.org.mk/amc/contact., where it is mandatory to provide the user's name. When submitting a complaint, the user is required to fill out the Form available on the website as an online form.

We require users to contact our customer support at +389-0800-800-88 if, after a transaction based on toll payment service, the user has any objections related to the amount of the toll charged.

A response to the submitted complaint will be provided in writing or electronically within the legally prescribed deadline, or no later than eight days from the date of receipt of the complaint in written or electronic form. The deadline for resolving the complaint is 15 days from the moment of its submission.

Right of User to Terminate the Contract

The user can terminate the contract by submitting a Request for Termination of the Contract to the email address: patarina@roads.org.mk.

PE State Roads is obliged to inform the client of the receipt of the Request for Contract Termination electronically. The deadline for submitting the Request for Contract Termination is 14 days, counted from the moment of concluding the Contract, or successful user registration.

If the user submits a request for contract termination and exercises their right in accordance with the General Terms and Conditions and the Consumer Protection Law, it is considered that the contract was not concluded.

If the user exercises the right to terminate the contract, as described in the General Terms and Conditions, they are obliged to settle all debts to PE State Roads for the services provided until the moment when the user notifies the service provider that they have exercised the right to terminate the contract.

Refund

In case the user's complaint results in the approval of a refund of certain funds, and the user has made a payment to any of the payment cards, either partially or in full, regardless of the reason for the refund, the Public Enterprise is obliged to make the refund.

Changes to General Terms and Conditions

PE State Roads aims to make the TOLL FOR ALL application as useful and functional as possible, and for this reason, the General Terms and Conditions and rules will be periodically updated and published as such on the official website, becoming immediately valid.

PE State Roads has the right and obligation to amend and supplement the General Terms and Conditions and the price list to align them with changes to the Law on Public Roads, the Decision on the amount and manner of toll collection (toll), other binding general acts, its business policy, and the needs of the users. PE State Roads will inform users of the amendments to the General Terms and Conditions and the price list through the official website.

Contact Us

In case of any questions or suggestions regarding the General Terms and Conditions for using the TOLL FOR ALL application, feel free to contact us via email: patarina@roads.org.mk and contact@roads.org.mk

Users who wish to purchase or configure an TOLL FOR ALL device can contact PE State Roads at the following email address: patarina@roads.org.mk.
