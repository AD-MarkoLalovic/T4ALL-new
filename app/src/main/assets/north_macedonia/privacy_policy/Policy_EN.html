Statement on Security of Online Payments and Protection of Confidential Transaction Data

The confidentiality of personal data is protected and secured when making payments through the web portal using an advanced system for securely accepting payment cards over the internet. This system ensures complete card and user personal data confidentiality from the moment they are entered into the payment gateway form. Payment data is transmitted encrypted from the web browser to the bank that issued the payment card. State Road Public Enterprise never comes into contact with the complete payment card data of its users.

The payment data entry form is secured with SSL transport encryption with the highest security. All stored data is additionally protected with encryption using a cryptographic device according to FIPS 140-2 level 3. The bank's payment gateway meets all security requirements for online payments prescribed by leading card brands, and it operates in accordance with the PCI DSS Level 1 standard, the highest security standard in the payment card industry. The bank considers all collected information confidential and treats it accordingly. The information is used solely for the purposes it was intended for. All user data is considered sensitive and completely secure, and their privacy is protected with state-of-the-art protective mechanisms. Only data necessary for performing transactions is collected in accordance with prescribed and stringent online payment procedures. Security controls and operational procedures applicable to our infrastructure ensure immediate system confidentiality. Additionally, through strict access controls, regular security monitoring, and deep inspections to prevent network failures and planned implementation of information security provisions, they continuously maintain and improve the security level of data protection from cards.

Data security for payments is guaranteed by the card payment processor, Halk Bank, so the entire payment process is conducted through the bank's page, and payment card data is not accessible to the State Road Public Enterprise system.
